Algorithm Implementation & Business Applications

Centrality Measures

  • PageRank: Customer influence scoring for referral programs and VIP identification
  • Betweenness Centrality: Network bridge customers connecting different segments
  • Degree Centrality: Direct connection analysis for network reach assessment
  • Eigenvector Centrality: Influence based on connection to other influential nodes

Community Detection

  • Louvain Algorithm: Natural customer groupings for targeted marketing campaigns
  • Connected Components: Isolated network segments and fraud ring identification
  • Triangle Counting: Network density analysis for community strength assessment
  • Label Propagation: Dynamic community evolution tracking over time
